Our Uniquely Human Approach

Both tracks are built on three core beliefs that make our cohorts fundamentally different.

1. The Cohort is the Catalyst.

We believe the primary agent of change is the cohort itself. The real magic is generated by the collective wisdom and shared experience of your peers.

2. The Desirability of Problems.

We reframe challenges not as obstacles to be eliminated, but as the essential fuel for growth and innovation.

3. Interpersonal Relationship Learning.

Our core methodology is learning through relationship, in real-time, building the emotional intelligence required to lead effectively.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you facilitate a cohort for our entire company?

Absolutely. We can create multiple Intact Team Cohorts across different departments or run a larger-scale program. Contact us for enterprise solutions.

How do you ensure confidentiality in the Individual & Community Cohorts?

All members sign a confidentiality agreement to create a safe space for sharing real challenges. Cohorts are also curated to include non-competing organizations.

Why offer gender-specific cohorts?

We recognize that some individuals find unique value and psychological safety in single-gender environments for certain discussions. It’s an option, not a requirement, designed to support diverse comfort levels and learning preferences.

What if I have to miss a session?

Life happens! Sessions are recorded for review (with cohort permission), and the group dynamic is designed to bring you back into the fold seamlessly.
